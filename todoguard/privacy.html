TodoGuard Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

TodoGuard is designed with privacy as a core principle. This app is built to work fully offline and does not collect, track, or transmit your personal data.

Data Collection

TodoGuard does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Specifically:

  • No account creation is required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed
  • No usage data is transmitted
  • No cloud services are used

All tasks and app data remain entirely on your device.

Internet & Network Access

TodoGuard does not require an internet connection to function.

If you choose to submit feedback using the in-app feedback option:

  • Your message is sent via your device's email client
  • The email is initiated explicitly by you
  • No data is transmitted automatically by the app
  • TodoGuard does not store or process submitted messages

Notifications

TodoGuard may send optional local notifications for reminders if you explicitly enable them.

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can enable or disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

Biometric & Device Access

If enabled, TodoGuard may use device-level biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to protect access to the app.

  • Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS
  • TodoGuard does not access, store, or transmit biometric information

Third-Party Services

TodoGuard does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

TodoGuard does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page.
